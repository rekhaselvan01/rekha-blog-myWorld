THORNY ROSE!! WHY DO YOU HAVE TO BE SO BEAUTIFUL??

I wish you were not so beautiful
Then I would not be drawn to you
I would not have learnt that you have thorns all over you
Wish I could only see your beautiful side
Wish I could totally ignore your thorny side
But it has impossible for me to do that


So now I have no choice but to stay far
I stay far and admire your beauty
It is impeccable and pulls me
I have no control
Why do you have to be so beautiful?


Why do you have such thorns?
It is beyond my comprehension
How can something so beautiful have thorns
I am still in shock; how could this be?
But I know through experience that you are very thorny
For you have pricked me and poked far too deep
For I have bleed so hard from your thorns


Even though your thorns have hurt me
I still admire your beauty
Is this madness or foolishness
Is this loving your worst enemy
Even when you know they are plotting to kill you
How can such a strange feeling arise and torture me?
All my life I have completely loved someone
Or I have completely hated them


But never been in such a pathetic state
Loving your worst enemy
Admiring the worst that you could find on earth
How could such a feeling exist?
Staying far from your thorns has stopped the bleeding
But I am still too damaged and hurting


But I got faith in myself
For I shall recover and heal
For I shall accept you and let you go
For I shall forgive you and forget you
Goodbye my beautiful thorny rose!.
