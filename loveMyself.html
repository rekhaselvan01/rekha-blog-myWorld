LOVE MYSELF JUST THE WAY I AM

It’s just another sleepless night. Many nights have passed in this manner and rather than complaining and seeing it as misfortune, I am learning to see it as a blessing. It makes me feel alive and allows me to be myself. I have always wanted to find ways to make me happy and come up with solutions and methods. But I really don’t want to do that anymore. I am perfect the way I am and more beautiful than ever. I don’t want to compare myself to others who seem to be happy or peaceful. I choose to be myself irrespective of the state of my mind.


Wanting to be happy and peaceful has made me more unhappy and miserable. Let me embrace the imperfection of my life and situation. I don’t want to change it anymore. This is who I am and there is nothing wrong with it. Each person is different, and I am like this, and I like this way.


Let the situation be like this and let everything around me be the same too. I don’t want to change anything but simply embrace it all and let it go. I am only going to live on this earth for a short while, I don’t want to scrutinize myself and keep wondering “why am I like this?” instead want to be happy that I am like this.


Let me be the way I am, also allow others to be the way they want. It is for my own sake I want to do this. All that matters is, “Am I living to my fullest and am I doing what my heart wants?”. If I am doing that nothing else matters. A whole lot of imperfections combine to make this perfect being that I am, it’s time to let go off the picture-perfect life.
