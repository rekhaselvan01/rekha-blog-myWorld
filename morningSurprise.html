MORNING SURPRISE

Woke up to this beautiful morning.
How lovely is the half moon,
With clouds passing over it
I look down and the sun shines through the woods right behind my house
What a heavenly sight?
How blessed am I?


In this precious moment right now
I leave behind all my worries
I leave behind all my grief
I leave behind all my fears
I leave behind all my pain
I leave behind all my regrets


I say hello to new beginnings
I say hello to life
I say hello to the morning sun
I say hello to the tweeting birds
I say hello to the unknown
I say hello to everyone and everything


Life is beautiful
It’s undeniable
It’s precious
It’s simple
Let’s live and let live!!!!!!!!
