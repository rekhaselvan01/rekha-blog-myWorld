YOU HAVE NOTHING

You had someone who loved you more than their life. You lost them.
You had someone who adored you day and night. You lost them.
You had someone who admired you. You lost them.
You had someone who trusted you with all their might. You lost them.


You had someone who wanted you more than anything in their life. You lost them.
You had someone who valued you like a priced possession. You lost them.
You had someone who was so innocent to believe all your lies. You lost them.
You had someone who admired your intellectual beauty. You lost them.


You had someone who came running to you even when you tortured them. You lost them.
You had someone who thought of you day and night. You lost them.
You had someone who would sacrifice their own beautiful self for your sake. You lost them.
You had someone who loved you with every single cell of their body and their whole mind. You lost them.


You had someone who would listen to your stories and fall even harder. You lost them.
You had someone who would analyze the things you do, analyze everything about you and fall some more like it was not enough. You lost them.
You had someone who would give in to all your desires and wishes even if it was killing them. You lost them.
You had someone who trusted you with every single cell of theirs, they would not believe in anyone else but you. You lost them.


You had someone who simply loved you for no reason but just for who you are. You lost them.
You had someone who would completely let go of their ego and identity and submit to you for love. You lost them.
You had someone who would do anything for you unconditionally with no expectations whatso ever. You lost them.
You had someone who was so innocent and beautiful like a flower. You lost them.


You lost them because you lied to them a million times. You had a million chances to come clean, but you lied endlessly.
You lost them because you never really loved them, not even for a nano second. It was all one big game.
You lost them because you broke their trust. You hurt them deep enough for them to die.


You lost them because you played with their feelings and used them to fulfill your needs.
You lost them because you scammed them. You pretended to be someone else.
You lost them because you took advantage of their innocence and fearful nature.


Despite all this nonsense, despite being ruined by you, they still became more beautiful
All this trauma brought some sense into their brain, and they finally understood their own true value.
All this made them mature enough to finally see their own worth and respect their own self.
All this made them resilient enough to see their own strength, physical, mental, and emotional strength.


All this made them stronger than ever and wiser than before.
All this made them more accepting and forgiving than before.
All this made them more playful and innocent and light-hearted than before.
All this made them appreciate the things and people around them more than before.


All this made them grateful and satisfied than before.
All this made them forgetful and forgiving than before.
All this made them more powerful, simple, and natural than before.


All I have to say is “THANK YOU” for coming into my life and making my life better.
All I have to say is “THANK YOU” for making me realize my own value and strength.
All I have to say is “THANK YOU” for making me dispassionate and free.
All I have to say is “THANK YOU” for beating away all the undesirable things in me.
All I have to say is “THANK YOU” for making me wiser and better.
